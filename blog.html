Place Must Visit

Goa, a tiny emerald land

I have visited in october 2016

Just had to share my views with anyone who is travelling first time to Goa. I have just returned from one of the best holidays that I have ever had. Four of us went (all first timers), and we all fell in love with the place. It was everything that I expected and more!! I had booked a villa in Candolim which was fantastic, just a short walk up a dirt track off the main road. I found the Goan people to be the most polite people I have ever met. They may be poor but they do not lack manners. The food was fantastic (had curry every day)!! There was plenty to choose from as one in our party was not a curry lover. It was all so cheap and for me especially the best curries that I have ever tasted. We went on a couple of trips. The waterfall trip was fantastic, and we ended that day by riding an elephant. The weather was brilliant, blue skies evryday. The only downside of the whole holiday was the flight, which I had already read about on this site. It was the Monarch flight from Gatwick. It was on time both ways,

review on the movie

frozen2
excellent movie!!

When people ask me what's my favorite Disney movie, I genuinely have no idea, but after watching Frozen 2 this week with my sisters, I can say with full confidence that my favorite Disney movie is Frozen 2. The first and foremost thing I love and admire is the love Anna show to Elsa, which is the best example of sister love. She always looks after her, checking on her, and do her best to protect her! I love how that is the center of the movie. It's a little bit different wherein other movies, the center is a romantic love, which I enjoy too. It's just this is different. The next thing I love is the power that both Elsa and Anna have in the movies. In the end, is they who save the day and rely little on strong men. Of course, there are men who helped them, especially Anna when she destroys the damages, but to me, this is realistic since a lot of physical strength is needed. Besides, she was leading them because she had the answers. I think Anna is much stronger in the second movie than the first.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling. The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The main story arc concerns Harry's struggle against Lord Voldemort, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal, overthrow the wizard governing body known as the Ministry of Magic and subjugate all wizards and Muggles (non-magical people).

Diary of a Wimpy Kid

Diary of a Wimpy Kid

Diary of a Wimpy Kid is an American children's book series and media franchise created by American author and cartoonist Jeff Kinney. The series follows Greg Heffley, a middle-schooler who illustrates his daily life in a diary. Kinney spent eight years working on the book before showing it to a publisher.
